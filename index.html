Who we are

We are building a world of possibilities for children with autism

The Zain Foundation is a charitable organization working towards building a better tomorrow for individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). Since our inception on June 11th, 2015, to honour the memory of a courageous 15-year-old boy who lost his battle with cancer the previous year, we have passionately advocated for the inclusion and support of the autism community.

Our vision is to create a holistic residential facility in Bhubaneshwar, Odisha, where adolescents and adults with autism can live happily, healthily, and with dignity. As the incidence of autism has risen from 1 in 10,000 children in 1971 to 1 in 38 children today globally, it has become more important than ever to create a safe space where individuals with autism can thrive.

We have been actively engaging in advocacy work since our inception in 2015 by organizing rallies, workshops, and exhibitions to promote the inclusion of individuals with autism in all aspects of social life. We hold annual fundraisers to raise funds for our expenses and have received tremendous support from our growing community of well-wishers.

What we do

Our activities and initiatives

  • Residential Infrastructure Development

  • Advocacy

  • Fundraising

  • Sensory and Vocational Activities

Residential Infrastructure Development

  • We are committed to building a holistic residential facility for adults and adolescents with autism.
  • We aim to provide a safe and nurturing environment where individuals with autism can live with dignity, compassion and independence.
  • Our long-term goal is to create a model for residential infrastructure development that can be replicated in other parts of the world.

Advocacy

  • We believe that individuals with autism have a right to be included in every area of social participation.
  • Our foundation engages in advocacy work by organizing rallies, workshops and exhibitions for the autism community.
  • Our goal is to create awareness about autism, reduce stigma and promote social inclusion.

Fundraising

  • We rely on donations and fundraising events to support our programs and initiatives.
  • Our annual fundraising event is a highlight of our calendar, and we appreciate the generous support of our well-wishers.

Sensory and Vocational Activities

  • Our foundation building is a spacious and inviting space designed for sensory and vocational activities.
  • We offer a range of activities that are tailored to the unique needs and abilities of individuals with autism.
  • Our aim is to provide individuals with autism with opportunities for personal growth, skill development and socialization
